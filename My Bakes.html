My Bakes

Besides baking Chinese New Year cookies with my mum, I do bake many other kinds of desserts. I especially love baking muffins and macarons. Baking makes me happy and I often bake when I am stressed and need a break. Although it can be tiring at times, I think it is a very therapeutic activity.

I often bake a lot to share with my friends and family. It is always very heartwarming to see my friends and family enjoying my bakes. I usually bake almond cookies during Chinese New Year. Not only do I bake enough for my family and for when relatives come and visit, I also bake many more for my friends. I also often bake brownies for my friends.

Although I mostly bake sweet desserts such as macarons and cookies, I have also tried to bake bread, tarts and pies. Of course, not all my bakes are successful. Sometimes, they turn out overcooked, soggy, or just falls apart. While it is sad when my bakes turn out bad, I will always try to analyse the problem and try again. To others it may be tiring but to me, it is a very fun process. When I see my bakes turn out good and delicious, I always feel a sense of accomplishment and happiness.
