My Inspirations

I always try to bake something new, trying different recipes each time.

Some of the time, what I bake is based on the ingredients I have at home. For instance, when there are overriped bananas, I would bake banana crumble muffins. Or, if there are many earl grey tea bags left, I would bake earl grey macarons or biscuits.

Most of the time, I look at Youtube videos for inspiration and ideas for what to bake. Some of the channels that I often visit are Cooking Tree, Nino's Home and HidaMari Cooking. Not only are their recipes delicious, but also easy to follow. Their videos are also very relaxing to watch. Below are some of the videos that I have followed and have baked delicious bakes from.



I also enjoy watching cooking shows such as Masterchef. Besides the entertainment value such shows gives, it is also fascinating to see participants cook incredible dishes and bakes. One of the most memorable contestant for me is Reynold Poernomo. He never fails to impress, has many creative ideas that are out of the box and creates not only beautiful but also delicious dishes and desserts.


Life is just better with fresh bakes 😋
